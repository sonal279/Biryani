Recipe

Step :1
Take a fry pan add some ghee then add cumin seed ,hing and khada masal like cloves ,cardimum then add onion and ginger garlic paste ,fry it untill onion change its colour to light brown then add your seasonal vegies ,add salt and leave it for 5-10 minutes then add masala and lastly curd now your curry is ready.
Step:2
Boil water in a cotainer add some ghee and pinch of salt ,wash basmati rice properly and put it in boiling water for some time untill rice is half cooked.
Step:3
Now we have to give dum to our biryani put rice and curry in layer and add milk and dry fruits above each layer and kewra water if you have cover the container properly you can use flour dough and leave it at low flame for 10 minutes and your yummy üòã biryani is ready enjoy it and give me feedback ‚ò∫
